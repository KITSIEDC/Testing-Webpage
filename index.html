What is KITS IEDC?

KITS IEDC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre) is an initiative to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students. Supported by government and academic institutions, it provides mentorship, resources, and funding to transform ideas into startups.

Why Join KITS IEDC?

- Access to industry mentors and experts.
- Funding opportunities for student projects.
- Networking with like-minded innovators.
- Participation in national and international competitions.
