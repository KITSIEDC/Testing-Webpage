About KITS IEDC

KITS IEDC is the **Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre** of **KITS**. Our mission is to **foster innovation** by providing students with resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities. We aim to **empower students** to create innovative solutions and launch successful startups.
