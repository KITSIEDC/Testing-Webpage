Welcome to KITS IEDC

KITS Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) is a dynamic platform that fosters creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship among students and aspiring entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

At KITS IEDC, we strive to cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset, enabling students to transform their innovative ideas into successful ventures. We provide mentorship, training, and incubation support to budding entrepreneurs.

Opportunities & Support

Through workshops, hackathons, networking events, and funding opportunities, KITS IEDC empowers students with the skills and resources required to build sustainable businesses and contribute to the global startup ecosystem.
